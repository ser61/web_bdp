Our History

Where We Started

10 February 1960

The origins of Memorise date back to August 8, 1907. Founded by Charles W. Iles, Ira Moller and Ardis Peterson, Memorise opened its doors at 575 S Kenwood Ave 3 in Chicago, IL. The funeral home continued under that management until 1979 when Mr. Iles became the sole owner.

Since the 1980s, our network of funeral homes has been extensively growing. In 1981, Charles W. Iles together with LeRoy Dunn established the funeral home at the old residence at 805 Pleasant Street in Los Angeles, CA.

Later, Mr. Dunn put into realization a dream which had become his life ambition – developing the most modern funeral network in the country and offering high-quality funeral services.

What Sets Us Apart

10 February 1984

Memorise NC, a member of the Memorise network in Apex, North Carolina, has served area families since 1982 with unmatched services and compassionate, professional care. The funeral home became a part of our network in 1987.

Memorise has been providing funeral, cemetery and cremation services for families in Chicago, IL, since 1907. For more than a century, our commitment has remained unchanged and yet, our funeral homes, cemeteries and cremation services have always responded to the changing needs of the community.

Memorise occupies 125 acres five miles east of downtown, situated on a slight hill with an ocean view.
